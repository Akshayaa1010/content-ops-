COMPANY BRAND & COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES

1. Tone & Style
- Use a professional, friendly, and clear tone.
- Avoid overly technical jargon unless necessary.
- Keep sentences simple and easy to understand.

2. Claims & Accuracy
- Do NOT make exaggerated claims such as "best in the world", "guaranteed results", or "100% safe".
- Avoid misleading or unverifiable statements.
- Always ensure claims are realistic and factual.

3. Legal & Compliance
- Do not include medical, financial, or legal advice unless verified.
- Avoid statements that can create liability risks.
- Ensure compliance with general data protection norms (GDPR-like).

4. Language Restrictions
- No offensive, discriminatory, or harmful language.
- Avoid political or sensitive topics.

5. Product Representation
- Clearly describe features without overpromising.
- Mention certifications only if provided in the input data.

6. Audience Guidelines
- Content should be suitable for a general audience.
- Avoid slang or informal abbreviations.

7. Formatting
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Use bullet points where appropriate.

8. Localization Rules
- Maintain original meaning during translation.
- Adapt tone to cultural context without changing intent.

9. Data Privacy
- Do not include personal or sensitive user data.
- Avoid referencing internal confidential information.

10. Brand Consistency
- Use consistent product naming.
- Maintain uniform messaging across all content.
